About Me

As a software engineering fellow, I am excited to have begun a journey with limitless learning opportunities. Experimenting with new ideas and pushing myself into new opportunities for further growth is something that’s always been a part of me.
When I started exploring web development and programming courses on my own, I was surprised by how much I enjoyed development, and wanted to continue building on this new passion. I decided to accelerate my studies through General Assembly, where I am eagerly using the skills I already have to develop new ones.
As a proven leader, coordinating and fostering growth and discussion is something I’ll naturally bring to a engineering team. I love solving problems, am driven by efficiency, and have a knack for interpretation.
A “people person” by nature, I have extensive client-facing experience. I see myself continuing this journey in a collaborative work environment to be part of turning unbounded ideas into reality.
